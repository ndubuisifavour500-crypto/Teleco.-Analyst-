## Week 5 — Business Analytics Case Study

### Telco Customer Churn Analysis

**Objective:**
Analyze customer churn data to identify key drivers of attrition and provide actionable business recommendations.

**Dataset:**
- Name: Telco Customer Churn
- Source: Kaggle (IBM Dataset)
- Size: 7,043 rows x 21 columns
- Target Variable: Churn (Yes/No)

**Tools & Libraries:**
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

**Key Findings:**
- Overall churn rate: 26.5%
- Month-to-month contracts churn at 42.7% vs 2.8% for two-year contracts
- Fiber optic customers churn at 41.9% despite being the premium service
- Electronic check users churn at 45.3% — highest of any payment method
- New customers (0–12 months) are the most vulnerable to churn

**Recommendations:**
1. Incentivize long-term contract upgrades
2. Implement an early customer retention program
3. Investigate fiber optic customer dissatisfaction
4. Bundle security add-ons into base plans
5. Promote automatic payment methods
6. Develop a senior citizen retention strategy

**Deliverables:**
- Jupyter Notebook (full analysis + visualizations)
- Business Analytics Report (Word)
- Business Presentation (PowerPoint)
- LinkedIn Post
